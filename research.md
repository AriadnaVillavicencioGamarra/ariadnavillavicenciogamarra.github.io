#### Research Assistant Experience

**Whitman College, Department of Economics**

##### Research Assistant for Dr. Wisnu Sugiarto

Recipient of the Louis B. Perry Summer Research Award. Conducted exploratory research in Utah on the relationship between alcohol laws and the socioeconomic status of drivers, as part of the preliminary work supporting Dr. Wisnu Sugiarto's paper, ["Drink…then drive away: The effects of lowering the blood alcohol concentration in Utah"](https://doi.org/10.1002/hec.4842) (*Health Economics*, 2024). Conducted literature review, data analysis, and visualization of public crash data for Utah and neighboring states using Python. Found that, following the 2019 implementation of Utah's lower blood alcohol concentration law, the average vehicle age increased and accident rates declined for two years, suggesting possible underreporting of property-damage-only (PDO) crashes.
